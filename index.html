What We Offer

Our Services

01

Gas Turbine Performance Models

Development of comprehensive, physics-based gas turbine performance models that accurately simulate engine behavior across the full operating envelope. Our models support both design-point and off-design analysis, incorporating compressor and turbine component maps, combustion characteristics, and customer-specified extraction flows.

Deliverables include validated cycle decks, station-by-station thermodynamic data, and parametric performance maps suitable for integration into larger system simulations.

NPSS Cycle Decks Component Maps Python Off-Design Analysis Aeroderivative
02

Gas Turbine Cycle Conceptual Design

Thermodynamic cycle analysis and conceptual design studies for gas turbine configurations including aeroderivative conversions to stationary power generation, novel extraction configurations, and free power turbine integration.

We support early-stage feasibility assessments through to preliminary design, providing cycle efficiency estimates, thermal performance projections, and comparative trade studies to guide critical design decisions.

Thermodynamic Analysis Trade Studies Power Generation Free Power Turbine Feasibility
03

Gas Turbine Performance Data Analysis

Rigorous analysis of measured engine performance data to assess operational health, quantify performance degradation, validate simulation models, and identify optimization opportunities. We apply statistical analysis and physics-based interpretation to extract meaningful insights from complex datasets.

Deliverables include performance benchmarking reports, degradation trend analysis, model validation summaries, and engineering recommendations to support operational and maintenance decisions.

Who We Are

About GTC

Engineering Expertise
You Can Trust

Gas Turbine Consulting, LLC is a specialized engineering consultancy focused exclusively on gas turbine performance. We bring deep technical expertise to performance modeling, thermodynamic cycle design, and data analysis — delivering precise, defensible engineering results to clients in the power generation and industrial sectors.

Our work is grounded in rigorous thermodynamic fundamentals and hands-on experience with the tools and methods used by leading gas turbine OEMs and operators. We work directly with client engineering teams to solve complex performance challenges efficiently and with engineering integrity.

  • Aeroderivative and industrial gas turbine performance
  • NPSS-based cycle modeling and simulation
  • Python-based performance analysis tooling
  • Component map development and validation
  • Combustion and emissions analysis
  • Power generation cycle optimization
  • Engineering documentation and reporting
Our Approach
We deliver focused, high-quality engineering analysis — not boilerplate reports. Every engagement is handled with direct technical involvement, clear communication, and a commitment to defensible, reproducible results.
Industry Focus
Power generation, aeroderivative conversions, and industrial gas turbine applications. We specialize in the intersection of flight-heritage engines and stationary power generation requirements.
Tools & Methods
NPSS, Python, Cantera thermodynamics, and proprietary simulation frameworks. We combine industry-standard tools with custom-developed methods to deliver analysis that goes beyond off-the-shelf solutions.
Engagement Model
We work as an extension of your engineering team — available for targeted studies, ongoing model development, or long-term consulting relationships tailored to your project needs.
